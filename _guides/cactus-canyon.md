## About the Table
Cactus Canyon (Bally, 1998) is a Wild West-themed pinball machine featuring a unique "lasso" skill shot mechanism and multiple multiballs. The table centers around gunfights, gold mining, and rescuing Polly Peril, with the ultimate goal of reaching High Noon wizard mode. It's known for its potential for massive bonus multipliers and multiball stacking opportunities.

## Skill Shots
The skill shot uses a spinning lasso mechanism - the ball circles around until it lands on a selected award. Key awards to target:
- **Lock** - Most valuable early game award to start building toward Gold Mine multiball
- **Quick Draw** - Starts a lucrative hurry-up mode immediately  
- **1M points** - Safe points option
- **Super Skill Shot**: Hold either flipper for 2 seconds while in shooter lane to upgrade the selected award

**Strategy**: The skill shot timing can be learned and is "potentially very lucrative" according to tournament players. Focus on Lock early game, Quick Draw for immediate scoring.

## Early Game Strategy  
1. **Skill shot for Lock** - Start building toward Gold Mine multiball immediately
2. **Hit the Gold Mine** to light lock, then lock balls when lit
3. **Complete upper left rollovers** (via left orbit shots) to build bonus multiplier - this is crucial as bonus can be massive
4. **Start Quick Draw** when lit at inlanes by hitting side spot targets or via skill shot
5. **Avoid Polly Peril** early - it locks you into a mode that prevents other progress

**Key early shots**: Left orbit (feeds rollovers), Gold Mine, and Quick Draw drop targets when lit.

## Mid Game Strategy
**Primary focus: Gold Mine Multiball stacking**
- Gold Mine MB: All major shots (3 ramps + 2 loops) lit for 500k+ jackpots
- Each jackpot adds 250k to Motherlode value
- Every 5 jackpots increases Motherlode multiplier
- Cash Motherlode at mine during multiball only (resets to 250k after collection)
- **Tournament key**: Stack Showdown with Gold Mine by raising final Quick Draw target, starting another multiball, then completing the Quick Draw

**Secondary scoring**:
- **Combos**: Ramps and loops build up to 4-way combos worth 300k max (need 10 total for High Noon)
- **Showdown preparation**: Complete Quick Draws (4 total needed) - each worth 500k-2M based on rank
- **Bonus multiplier**: Continue hitting left orbit during multiballs to max out bonus X

## Late Game Strategy
**End-of-ball priorities**:
1. **Cash any built Motherlode** during Gold Mine multiball
2. **Focus on bonus multiplier** - can be enormous, especially during multiball when left orbit diverter stays closed
3. **Complete any active Quick Draw** for guaranteed points
4. **Avoid risky shots** to Polly Peril or Bart Brothers unless specifically needed

**Conservative play**: Trap up and focus on safe orbit shots to build bonus rather than risky ramp shots.

## Multiballs
1. **Gold Mine Multiball** (Primary scorer)
   - Lock 3 balls at mine when lit
   - 5 jackpot shots (3 ramps, 2 loops) worth 500k+ each
   - Builds Motherlode value (250k per jackpot, multiplier every 5 shots)
   - Cash Motherlode at mine during multiball

2. **Showdown Multiball** 
   - Complete 4 Quick Draws to start
   - Hit drop targets for 300k jackpots
   - Each completed set of 4 targets = add-a-ball (unlimited in original ROM, limited to 2 in tournament ROM)
   - **Stacking opportunity**: Can combine with Gold Mine/Stampede

3. **Stampede Multiball**
   - Hit all major shots 3 times each to start
   - All shots worth 250k, one roving shot worth 500k
   - In tournament ROM: Hitting roving shot repeatedly without other shots multiplies value up to 6x (3M max)

## Key Tips
- **Left orbit during multiball** - Always feeds rollovers due to closed diverter, builds massive bonus multiplier
- **Motherlode timing** - Can only cash during Gold Mine multiball, not after it ends
- **Shootout technique** - Watch for the Bad Guy target that doesn't flash during darkness - that's the one to hit
- **Multiball stacking** - Start other multiballs while final Quick Draw target is raised, then complete Quick Draw
- **Ranking system** - Higher ranks increase Quick Draw values (Marshall starts at 2M vs Stranger at 500k)
- **Tournament ROM differences** - Bonus X capped at 24x, only 2 add-a-balls in Showdown, but Stampede worth more
- **Dangerous shots** - Avoid Polly Peril unless specifically needed (locks you into 5 ramp shots)
- **High Noon requirements** - Need 10 combos, Motherlode collection, 3 Bart Brothers defeated, Stampede started, and Showdown started
