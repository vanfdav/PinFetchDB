## About the Table
Williams Comet (1985) is an early solid-state machine themed around an amusement park comet ride. Notable for being the first pinball machine to feature a single shot worth one million points, it features a unique flipper layout with inverted inlanes/outlanes on the left side and no inlane on the right. The table includes a center Comet ramp, cycle jump ramp, corkscrew ramp, and drop target banks.

## Skill Shots
Plunge lightly to route the ball through the 1-9-8-6 lanes at the top of the playfield. Completing these lanes lights a timed playfield multiplier: 2x on ball 1, 3x on ball 2, and 5x on ball 3. This is the most important skill shot as the 5x multiplier on ball 3 enables the million-point shot at the cycle jump ramp.

## Early Game Strategy
Focus on completing the skill shot lanes (1-9-8-6) immediately. Work on the Duck and Rabbit drop target banks to light the center Comet ramp for higher scoring (30k, 50k, 100k progression). The cycle jump ramp starts each ball lit and is worth 200k base - shoot it early if multiplier is active. After shooting the jump ramp, it must be re-lit by going through the left inlane.

## Mid Game Strategy
Complete both drop target banks to light the center Comet ramp for escalating values (30k → 50k → 100k → 100k repeating). The lit Comet ramp also provides extra ball opportunities. Build bonus multiplier by hitting the bull's-eye target before drop targets reset (1st hit = 2x, 2nd = 3x, 3rd = 4x, 4th = 5x, then 20k per additional hit). Stack scoring opportunities when possible - if playfield multiplier is active, prioritize the 200k jump ramp hole.

## Late Game Strategy  
Ball 3 is crucial for the million-point strategy. Complete 1-9-8-6 lanes for 5x playfield multiplier, then immediately shoot the lit cycle jump ramp's 200k hole (200k × 5x = 1 million points). You have approximately 20 seconds of multiplier time. If multiplier runs out, focus on safe center ramp shots and building end-of-ball bonus. Trap up and play conservatively once you've achieved your scoring objectives.

## Multiballs
Comet does not feature traditional multiball modes. Focus on single-ball play and maximizing scoring through multipliers and progressive ramp values.

## Key Tips
- **Million Point Shot**: Complete 1-9-8-6 on ball 3, then shoot the small 200k hole at back of cycle jump ramp during 5x multiplier
- **Jump Ramp Re-lighting**: After shooting jump ramp, ball must pass through left inlane to re-light it
- **Dangerous Layout**: Left side has inverted inlane/outlane - what looks like inlane actually drains the ball
- **Right Side Drain**: No inlane on right side, only outlane - use controlled flipper shots
- **Nudge Saves**: Left outlane allows nudge saves back into play
- **Progressive Scoring**: Center Comet ramp increases in value each shot when lit (30k → 50k → 100k)
- **Bonus Multiplier**: Hit bull's-eye drop target before banks reset to advance multiplier up to 5x
- **Safe Shots**: Center Comet ramp is safest repeatable scoring when lit
