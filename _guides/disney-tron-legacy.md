## About the Table
TRON: Legacy is a 2011 Stern pinball machine based on Disney's film sequel. The game features a unique spinning disc that randomizes ball paths, multiple stackable multiballs, and a complex wizard mode progression. The playfield uses blacklight effects with glowing Tron-style artwork that creates dramatic visual appeal during play.

## Skill Shots
The most reliable skill shot is a **soft plunge** that feeds the ball safely to the left flipper, setting up clean shots to either the Quorra target (left of disc) or the left ramp. This awards around 250K points with minimal risk. Advanced players can attempt a **power plunge to hit the Gem switch** directly off the plunge with precise power and nudging, but this carries significant drain risk. The **light cycle shot behind the pop bumpers** can also be achieved with a hard plunge if you need just one more shot to light Light Cycle Multiball.

## Early Game Strategy
Start every ball by **soft plunging for flipper control**. Your first priority should be **lighting Double Scoring from the TRON standup targets** on the left side, as this multiplies scoring for the entire game including Sea of Simulation awards. The TRON targets award random features, so hit them early to potentially get the crucial 2X scoring. Next, focus on **safe, repeatable shots to the left ramp and Quorra** to build up basic scoring and mode progression. Avoid risky shots to the spinning disc area early unless specifically needed for mode progression.

## Mid Game Strategy
The core tournament strategy revolves around the **"End of Line" combo system**. Any shot sequence that ends with the upper ramp feeding to the left flipper enables a jackpot at Flynn's Arcade scoop worth significant points. Focus on **stacking Light Cycle and Quorra multiballs**, as these can be combined for massive scoring opportunities. During multiball, prioritize **combo shots over individual jackpots** - for example, Left Ramp → Quorra → Side Ramp awards Super Jackpots. Light Cycle Multiball specifically rewards **any three-way combo with a Super Jackpot and mode completion**. Mode values typically start at 250K for jackpots with scoring differentiated in 1M increments.

## Late Game Strategy
If you've completed most modes, focus on **reaching Sea of Simulation** by completing all 9 central feature inserts. However, there's a powerful skip strategy: if you have Double Scoring active and multiple consecutive modes completed, the **Sea of Simulation completion bonus can be doubled**, making it extremely lucrative. During end-of-ball situations, **End of Line combos remain your highest-value repeatable shot**. Be aware that bonus multipliers can reach 6X-7X but timing a drain with 2X scoring active is unreliable in tournament play.

## Multiballs
**Light Cycle Multiball**: Started by hitting 4 shots to the light cycle ramp/orbit, then shooting the scoop. Awards 350K for lit shots, 750K for ramp combos, and 1.5M for double super jackpots via specific combo sequences.

**Quorra Multiball**: Started by shooting Quorra target left of the disc, then the scoop. Features **two add-a-balls available** by hitting the center standup bank 3 times (can be done twice per multiball). Has a long grace period - if only one ball remains, quickly shoot the center bank to continue multiball.

**Disc Multiball**: Requires collecting 10 discs from ramp shots, then hitting the recognizer bank 3 times, then the spinning disc for super jackpot. Cannot be stacked with other multiballs but offers a restart if you don't collect the super jackpot.

## Key Tips
- **End of Line combos are your bread and butter** - any sequence ending with upper ramp → left flipper → scoop for big jackpots
- **Double Scoring from TRON targets applies to everything** including Sea of Simulation - prioritize this early
- **Quorra Multiball add-a-balls have generous grace periods** - shoot center bank quickly when down to one ball
- **Three-way combos during Light Cycle MB instantly complete the mode** with Super Jackpot
- **Sea of Simulation skip with Double Scoring active** can be worth massive points if you have multiple modes completed
- **Avoid the spinning disc area** unless specifically required - it's unpredictable and risky
- **Right orbit during Sea of Simulation** lights Flynn's Arcade scoop for the next needed shot
- **Completing all modes before Sea of Simulation** awards instant 50M and starts Portal Multiball
- **Tournament mode typically reduces scoop awards** to 500K, making End of Line combos even more valuable
