## About the Table
Dune is a 2025 pinball machine manufactured by Barrels of Fun based on Denis Villeneuve's Dune films (2021 & 2024). The game features a revolutionary sandworm mechanism that can physically eat the ball, resource management with water and spice collection, and deep thematic integration with prophecy modes and harvester battles.

## Skill Shots
The game offers multiple skill shot options that award either spice or water resources. These resources are crucial for starting modes and multiballs, making skill shots particularly valuable in tournament play. Target the skill shots that award resources you need most for your current objectives. The plunge strength determines which resource you collect, so practice consistent plunger timing.

## Early Game Strategy
Prioritize collecting water and spice resources immediately - these are essential for starting major scoring opportunities. Focus on safe, repeatable shots to build your resource pool rather than attempting risky shots early. The day/night cycle affects scoring, so pay attention to the current phase. Lock balls in the Fedaykin Sand Trap when possible, as this enables one of the most valuable multiballs. Avoid the dangerous outlanes early unless you have Fedaykin protection activated.

## Mid Game Strategy
Start Harvester Battles when you have sufficient resources - completing these provides substantial scoring and leads to feeding Harkonnen to the sandworm for additional jackpots. The Prophecy modes (Awaken, Train, Fight, Rescue, Ride, Survive) are your primary point generators. Stack these with resource collection when possible. The center shot followed by left orbit is a key combo shot. Use the sandworm mechanism strategically - when it eats your ball, you get unique scoring opportunities and the dramatic ball release can set up advantageous positions.

## Late Game Strategy
Focus on completing started modes rather than beginning new ones. The end-of-ball bonus can be significant, especially if you've collected substantial water and spice. If you have a Fedaykin lock ready, let dangerous drains happen through the left outlane to activate the protection mechanism. Be conservative with risky shots unless you're behind and need to catch up - the game rewards consistent play over desperate attempts.

## Multiballs
**Fedaykin Multiball**: Lock balls in the sand trap (right side). This is the primary multiball and highly valuable for tournament play. Recent code updates require starting this during a Harvester Battle. Jackpots are available immediately on the first Fedaykin but require sand trap shots to light on subsequent attempts.

**Desert Power Multiball**: Details limited but appears to involve the sandworm mechanism. Look for opportunities to feed balls to the worm during regular play.

Additional multiballs are available but specific start conditions need further research as the game is very new.

## Key Tips
- The Fedaykin sand trap provides ball save protection when a ball drains down the left outlane - use this strategically
- Water and spice resources have maximum limits - don't over-collect when at capacity  
- The sandworm mechanism is both a scoring opportunity and potential ball save - don't fear it
- Completing Prophecy modes or Harvester battles provides balanced, substantial scoring
- The game rewards thematic play - following the story progression often leads to better scores
- Insert lighting is minimal but clear - pay attention to the small water (blue) and spice (red) lamps
- Day/night cycle affects scoring values - time your major shots accordingly
- Center shot to left orbit combo is a reliable scoring sequence
- Code is actively being updated - stay current with rule changes for tournament play
