## About the Table
Fish Tales is a Williams pinball machine from 1992 featuring a fishing theme with a talking fish character. The table centers around two main scoring strategies: Monster Fish hurry-ups from the center ramps and massive Super Jackpots during multiball. The playfield features boat-shaped ramps, a captive ball in the center, and Caster's Club ball locks on the right side.

## Skill Shots
The skill shot involves plunging the ball directly to Caster's Club on the right side of the playfield. This immediately locks a ball toward multiball and is the safest, most valuable skill shot available. The ball should feed to the left flipper after the plunge, making it relatively easy to execute consistently. There are no super skill shots available on this table.

## Early Game Strategy
Start every ball by attempting the skill shot to Caster's Club to immediately lock a ball. If you miss, focus on alternating the center boat ramps (left and right) to build toward Monster Fish. Make the right orbit shot to advance Long Cast awards and light video mode on the captive ball. Avoid the dangerous fish standups early unless you're very close to completing a Tale - they're risky shots that can lead to drains.

## Mid Game Strategy
The primary scoring comes from two paths: **Monster Fish** or **Super Jackpots**. For Monster Fish, alternate the six center ramp shots to light the hurry-up on the left orbit, starting at 20M and increasing to 50M maximum. For the multiball path, lock balls at Caster's Club, then during multiball immediately put another ball back in Caster's Club to light jackpots. Shoot the left orbit all the way to the back saucer for jackpots worth 20-40M+. After collecting 3 jackpots total, the captive ball lights for Super Jackpots worth 100M+ each hit.

## Late Game Strategy
Focus on completing either Monster Fish cycles or getting to Super Jackpots. If in multiball with Super Jackpots lit, prioritize hitting the captive ball repeatedly over all other shots - it can be worth 600M+ per hit. End-of-ball bonus is minimal compared to these major features, so don't play conservatively for bonus. If close to Feeding Frenzy (4 fish tales collected), the 30-second mode can be worth 45M+ and is relatively safe to attempt.

## Multiballs
**Caster's Club Multiball**: Lock 2-3 balls at Caster's Club on the right side. During multiball, immediately shoot Caster's Club again to lock balls and light jackpots on the left orbit. Collect 3 total jackpots to light Super Jackpots on the captive ball. Jackpots carry over between multiballs and multiply (2x, 3x, 4x, 5x, 6x) on subsequent multiball cycles. Super Jackpots can be collected repeatedly for 100M+ each hit until multiball ends.

## Key Tips
- **Super Jackpots are the highest scoring feature** - prioritize getting 3 jackpots to light the captive ball for 100M+ repeatedly
- **Left orbit shot** is the safest high-value repeatable shot for jackpots and Monster Fish collection
- **Monster Fish increases each time** - starts at 20M, maxes at 50M, making later attempts more valuable
- **Extra Ball target** on the boat is much easier to hit with a backhand from the right flipper
- **Video mode** is the 2nd Long Cast award and very valuable early - aim for far boats for maximum scoring and extra ball opportunity
- **Avoid fish standups** unless very close to completing a Tale - they're dangerous drain shots
- **Use flippers instead of the gun** in video mode for faster, more accurate shooting
- **Right orbit to captive ball** advances Long Cast awards - each successful loop is worth increasing points up to 5M
