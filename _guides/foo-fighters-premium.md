## About the Table
Foo Fighters is a 2023 Stern Premium pinball machine themed around the rock band's tour across America to defeat alien Overlords and save rock 'n' roll. The game features 15 iconic Foo Fighters songs, extensive multiball stacking opportunities, and a novel storyline with hand-drawn animations. Key features include the upper mini-playfield (Premium), captive UFO ball, and complex scoring through van modifications and city modes.

## Skill Shots
The crossover shot to the left provides the best skill shot opportunity. After collecting or missing other skill shots, the left crossover stays lit for a skill shot combo sequence. Shoot the crossover, then alternate between the upper loop and side ramp for up to 6 shots total. This combo sequence provides consistent points and is relatively safe.

For plunge options: soft plunge into the drop targets to instantly mod your van and boost your next mode, or plunge directly to the left outlane for bigger immediate points (though riskier). A very soft plunge into the Rock-O-Meter lane advances toward playfield multiplier.

## Early Game Strategy
Start with **New York mode** as you can progress using just the spinner, making it the safest city to complete first. Focus on lighting van modes by shooting white arrows to light the left ramp. Lock balls early before starting any van mode to maximize their benefit. 

Build UFO captive ball charges by hitting it a few times - you can hold up to 3 charges (yellow=1, green=2, red=3) and bring them into modes or multiballs. Save your first charge as UFO charges build 2x faster when you already have 1-2 charges.

## Mid Game Strategy
Prioritize the **best mode combinations**: Los Angeles/Seattle with Area 51, New York with Bot Frenzy, and Sonic Radio with New Orleans for maximum point scoring and multiball building. The side ramp scores double what the left and right ramps score during Los Angeles mode.

Focus on **2x Playfield activation**: shoot the Right Orbit into the Rock-O-Meter lane 3 times for 40 seconds of 2x scoring. Repeat during this timer for 3x Playfield. Stack this with raygun target multipliers - hit both lit inlane raygun targets via alley pass for a 4x shot (relight via side inlane rollovers).

Save Austin and DC multiballs until after completing all 6 cities to maximize their value - these are the highest-scoring opportunities in the game.

## Late Game Strategy
**Austin and DC multiballs** are your endgame scoring machines. Austin multiball can score 499+ million when properly set up after city completion. Focus on super jackpots during these modes for maximum points.

The left ramp becomes permanently 2x after finishing Roswell, making it an excellent late-game repeatable shot. Use UFO button charges strategically during multiballs - the button spots lit shots and can be crucial for difficult targets during multiball chaos.

Be conservative with dangerous shots like the upper orbit unless you have UFO charges to spot them safely.

## Multiballs
**Overlord Multiballs**: Started through various van modes. You can start Area 51 multiball during an Overlord multiball (but not vice versa). For upgrades, score 2 super jackpots or 1 2x super jackpot in the first Overlord, or 1 super jackpot in subsequent ones.

**Area 51 Multiball**: Can stack with Overlord modes. Score a super jackpot for the upgrade.

**Austin/DC Wizard Multiballs**: Save these for after completing all 6 cities. Austin can deliver 400-500+ million points. Focus entirely on super jackpots and add-a-balls.

**Bot Frenzy/Sonic Radio**: Secondary multiballs that work well stacked with city modes for combined scoring.

## Key Tips
- Left ramp is very backhandable and becomes permanently 2x after Roswell completion
- Soft plunge to flipper for a controlled free shot opportunity
- UFO button spots the highest value lit shot - save charges for difficult shots during multiballs
- Increase Bonus X by hitting the paddle target while drop targets are still standing
- Best early strategy: lock balls → start New York → build toward mode combinations
- Los Angeles side ramp scores double the other ramps - prioritize it during that mode
- Ray gun target strategy: alley pass to hit both lit targets for 4x scoring
- Start modes only after getting van modifications for enhanced scoring
- Austin/DC multiballs are your biggest tournament scoring opportunities - save them for last
