## About the Table
Funhouse is a 1990 Williams classic featuring the memorable talking head "Rudy" and a carnival funhouse theme. The game centers around advancing the clock to midnight to put Rudy to sleep for multiball, while collecting Mystery Mirror awards to reach Super Frenzy mode. Known for its simple yet strategic gameplay with high-scoring potential.

## Skill Shots
**Soft plunge** for a safe feed to the right flipper - this is the preferred tournament skill shot as it provides control and safety. The **Mirror skill shot** is available by shooting the Mystery Mirror on plunge, which is particularly valuable on your first ball to start working toward Quick Multiball. Avoid full-power plunges as they often lead to dangerous bounces through the bumpers.

## Early Game Strategy
**First 30 seconds priority:** Shoot the **Mystery Mirror** whenever lit to collect awards and work toward Super Frenzy. The mirror must be hit after Rudy says something to relight it. Use the **lock shot (hidden hallway)** or **Steps ramp** to feed the upper flipper for clean shots at Rudy to relight the mirror. Focus on safe, controlled play while advancing the clock toward 11:30 through various target hits.

## Mid Game Strategy
**Primary scoring path:** Work toward **multiball** by advancing clock to 11:30, then locking 2 balls and shooting Rudy's mouth. During multiball, shoot the **trapdoor for jackpots** (starting at 1M, increasing by 1M each collection) and use the **ramp to re-enable jackpots**. 

**Mystery Mirror strategy:** Collect all mirror awards to activate **Super Frenzy mode** - a timed 40-second mode worth **100K per switch hit**. The final mirror award is **Quick Multiball** which should be saved for last to stack with other modes.

**Key shots:** The **Steps ramp** and **lock shot** provide the best feeds to the upper flipper. The **Gangway shots** (orbit shots) can award up to **250K points** and possible **Extra Ball**.

## Late Game Strategy
**End-of-ball priorities:** If close to midnight, complete the multiball sequence for guaranteed big points. If close to completing mirror awards, prioritize **Super Frenzy** activation. During **Super Frenzy**, focus on rapid switch hits rather than difficult shots - every switch is worth **100K**. 

**Conservative play:** When ahead, use the lock shot to safely feed the upper flipper and avoid risky shots. The **left outlane** can serve as a ball save when you collect the "Open Steps" award - don't shoot the ramp immediately.

## Multiballs
**Midnight Multiball:** Advance clock to 11:30, lock 2 balls, shoot Rudy's mouth. Strategy: Shoot **trapdoor for jackpots** (1M base, +1M each), use **ramp to re-enable** jackpots. Focus on trapdoor shots as jackpots increase significantly.

**Quick Multiball:** Available as final Mystery Mirror award. Provides **3-ball play** with **unlimited millions on Rudy shots** plus normal jackpots at trapdoor. Best used after collecting other mirror awards for maximum stacking potential.

## Key Tips
- **Lock shot and Steps ramp** provide the cleanest feeds to upper flipper for Rudy shots
- **Soft plunge** every time - full plunges are dangerous and unpredictable  
- **Save Quick Multiball** as the last mirror award to stack with Super Frenzy
- **Super Frenzy adds 1M base value** to your frenzy scoring (total of 1.1M per switch)
- If you're nicknamed "Slick" by Rudy, you'll never get flashing Steps awards
- **Right outlane ball return:** If first switch hit is right rollover, you may get the ball back
- During multiball, **trapdoor jackpots increase by 1M each** - prioritize multiple collections
- **Gangway progressive** can reach 250K + Extra Ball - light via inlanes
- Use **nudging** to save center drains, especially during multiball
- **STEPS targets** completion lights the Steps ramp hole for Frenzy mode access
