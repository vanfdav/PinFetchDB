## About the Table
Ghostbusters Premium is a 2016 Stern pinball machine based on the classic movie franchise. The Premium features unique two-way magnetic ramps, an interactive moving Slimer target, and deep rule set with multiple scoring paths. Known for its complex mode structure and potential for massive scores through stacked multiballs and multipliers.

## Skill Shots
**Left Rollover (P):** Select this as your primary skill shot. It's the most consistent to hit and awards +3x bonus multiplier each time, which scales tremendously as your score grows. The skill shot is worth 10% of your current score + 1M base.

**Alternative Options:** If you already have a mode running, select the "K" rollover to light playfield multipliers, which can stack with Stay Puft for huge scoring. The right scoop can also be selected for an easier 2x multiplier when modes are active.

## Early Game Strategy
**Priority 1:** Hit the captive balls twice within 30 seconds to qualify locks at the left ramp. This is essential for multiball preparation.

**Priority 2:** Start collecting gears via the left ramp repeatedly. This builds toward a hurry-up on the Proton Pack targets that can be worth significant points.

**Priority 3:** Begin working on mode qualification by shooting the GHOST target and advancing through PKE lanes. Focus on the left scoop path (2 modes) as it's the safest route to "We Came, We Saw."

## Mid Game Strategy
**Main Scoring Path:** Work toward completing mode trees for "We Came, We Saw" mini-wizard mode. The left scoop tree requires only 2 modes, making it the most tournament-viable path. This starts a 2-ball multiball where all shots are lit for super jackpots.

**Multiplier Management:** Use the PKE lanes and bumpers to build bonus multipliers to 10x+ and get bonus holds. This can turn end-of-ball bonus from millions to billions.

**Stacking Opportunities:** If Stay Puft mode is available, build up multipliers by shooting Gozer first (aim for 3x-4x minimum), then activate Storage Facility multiball and keep it alive during the mode.

## Late Game Strategy
**Conservative Approach:** With a good score established, focus on safe left ramp shots to collect gears and build toward Proton Pack hurry-ups rather than risking dangerous center shots.

**Bonus Strategy:** Ensure bonus multipliers are maximized and held before draining. A 20x bonus with good base bonus can be worth several billion points.

**End-of-Ball:** If multiball is lit, consider starting it even late in the ball as the multiball restart can provide additional opportunities.

## Multiballs
**Storage Facility:** Lock 3 balls at the left ramp after qualifying via captive balls. Standard multiball with jackpots available throughout the playfield.

**We Came, We Saw:** Mini-wizard multiball (2-4 balls depending on completed tree) where all shots are super jackpots. Tournament exploit: trap one ball and repeatedly shoot left ramp from right flipper for consistent super jackpots.

**Mass Hysteria:** Main wizard mode - 4-ball multiball lasting 60 seconds with ghost-catching mechanic. Continues even down to single ball.

## Key Tips
- **Left ramp repeatability:** This is your safest consistent shot for gear collection and mode progression
- **Avoid the Slimer lane:** Extremely dangerous shot with minimal tournament reward
- **Playfield multiplier stacking:** Combine mode multipliers with playfield multipliers for shots worth 250M+
- **Ball save exploitation:** Short plunge to right flipper, shoot captive balls, then take skill shot
- **Competition mode quirk:** In Negative Reinforcement, you get a correct guess for every 10 ghosts earned, but your very next pick will always be wrong
- **Super loopins tip:** Get the ball into bumpers before hitting the ramp to add 10 seconds to the timer
- **PKE Frenzy scaling:** Can be worth 2-3 billion+ and scales better than other modes at high scores
