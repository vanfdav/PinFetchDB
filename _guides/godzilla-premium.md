## About the Table
Godzilla Premium (Stern, 2021) is a complex modern Stern featuring the iconic monster franchise. The Premium model includes unique features like the motorized building, retractable bridge ramp, and the innovative "Magna Grab" magnetic newton ball that can catch balls from five different paths. The game centers around battling various Kaiju monsters while managing multiple scoring stacks and features.

## Skill Shots
Multiple skill shots are available with varying difficulty and reward ratios:

**Short Plunge (Easiest)**: Plunge softly to hit the first switch scores 100,000 points and extends ball save time. This is the most consistent tournament choice.

**Super Skill Shot (High Risk/High Reward)**: Plunge behind the upper flipper, then shoot the scoop for 11,000,000 x2 and lights/collects an Ally. Extremely difficult but massive payoff.

**Skill Shot Stacking**: Each successful skill shot adds +1x multiplier to the next one. Consider short plunges on balls 1-2, then attempt super skill shot on ball 3 for maximum value.

## Early Game Strategy
**First 30 seconds priority:**
1. Make any skill shot for ball save extension
2. Immediately select Rodan as your ally (cycle with right flipper, shoot scoop) for 2x playfield scoring for 60 seconds
3. Start qualifying Kaiju battles: shoot both ramps, then scoop to light battles
4. Begin building locks for Godzilla Multiball: shoot both ramps, then building (3 shots to building starts multiball)

The Rodan ally's 2x playfield multiplier is crucial early - all subsequent scoring is doubled for a full minute, making it the highest-value immediate target.

## Mid Game Strategy
**Primary scoring focus: Kaiju Battles with 2x scoring**

1. **Gigan Battle (Recommended first battle)**: Easiest battle with good points. On Premium, when locks are lit, the right ramp feeds to left flipper making ramp combos repeatable.

2. **Heat Ray charging**: Hit center spinner 60 times to light Heat Ray. Each subsequent Heat Ray increases in value (60x, 120x, 180x, etc.). Use Heat Ray strategically during battles or multiballs - hold action button for 5 seconds to instantly collect all lit shots.

3. **Multiball stacking**: Start Godzilla MB (both ramps + building x3), then stack with Jet Fighter Attack, Tesla Strike, or other features during battles.

4. **Power-ups priority**: Always choose +5x time bonus as second power-up for quick battle completion with massive time bonuses.

## Late Game Strategy
**End-of-ball considerations:**
- On final ball, right outlane drain triggers Oxygen Destroyer (disabled in tournament play)
- Save Heat Ray for difficult shots or time-critical moments
- Focus on completing started modes rather than starting new ones
- Trap up during multiball to control ball flow and avoid drains
- Use "belly flop" method to safely activate Heat Ray without risk

**High-value closeouts:**
- Complete any active Kaiju battle for time bonus + 5x multiplier
- Cash out lit jackpots during active multiball
- Use Heat Ray to clear difficult shots like Maser Cannon or right-most powerline targets

## Multiballs
**Godzilla Multiball**: Both ramps + building (3x). On Premium, building motor creates dramatic ball release. Stack with other modes for maximum scoring.

**Mechagodzilla Multiball**: Use jump ramp to bash magnetic newton ball on chest. Premium features enhanced animations.

**Tank Attack/Bridge Attack**: Available on Premium. Bridge Attack features retractable animated bridge ramp.

**Add-a-balls**: 
- Right spinner (30 spins) qualifies add-a-ball for Godzilla MB
- Anguirus ally provides one-time add-a-ball button during any multiball

## Key Tips
- **Always select Rodan ally first** - 2x playfield scoring for 60 seconds is game-changing
- **Start with Gigan battle** - easiest completion with solid points, especially on Premium with lit locks
- **Center spinner is crucial** - 60 spins lights Heat Ray, which scales up significantly (60x, 120x, 180x)
- **Use Heat Ray strategically** - hold action button 5 seconds to collect all lit shots instantly
- **Mothra ally lights left outlane ball save** - useful insurance
- **Secret skill shot combo**: Super skill shot + scoop = 22M and ally collection
- **Avoid dangerous shots**: Left spinner during battles, difficult upper flipper shots unless necessary
- **Tournament note**: Oxygen Destroyer is typically disabled in tournament play
- **Premium advantage**: Lit locks make right ramp feed to left flipper for easier ramp combos
