## About the Table
Goldeneye is a 1996 Sega pinball machine based on the James Bond film. The table features a unique ball-capturing satellite dish, a gun-style plunger, and multiple multiballs. Key features include spelling GOLDENEYE for hurry-ups, five 007 Encounter modes, and both Tank and Satellite multiballs.

## Skill Shots
The skill shot is launched from the gun-style plunger. Aim for the right skill shot lane for safe ball control into the right flipper. There's also a super skill shot available by hitting specific targets during the plunge, though the exact mechanics vary by machine settings.

## Early Game Strategy
Focus on safe ramp shots to spell GOLDENEYE. Each ramp shot collects a letter, and completing the word starts valuable hurry-ups at all three ramps worth 50M each (counting down 1M per second to 20M). Hit the center standup target to light locks on the inner left ramp. Avoid starting Tank Multiball early - it's worth significantly fewer points than Satellite Multiball.

## Mid Game Strategy
**Satellite Multiball is the priority scoring opportunity.** Hit all green guard standups to award the points lit in front of the satellite (cycle value with white target under satellite). The far left ramp feeds to left inlane/Satellite area. Once Satellite Multiball starts, focus on building the jackpot value by hitting the right-center white standup to increase jackpot to 100M - this is easiest when down to 2-3 balls. Stack GOLDENEYE hurry-ups with multiball when possible for maximum scoring.

## Late Game Strategy
If both multiballs are available on ball 3 (common if you haven't locked balls), prioritize Satellite over Tank. Build combo values - consecutive ramp shots score combo bonus starting at 10M and increasing by 2M for each consecutive shot. The left ramp to Satellite combo doubles the combo value. Focus on safe, high-value shots rather than risky mode attempts.

## Multiballs
**Tank Multiball:** Lower-scoring multiball. Can be cancelled by quickly pulling the gun trigger after starting to instead attempt Satellite Multiball.

**Satellite Multiball:** The primary scoring feature. Started by shooting the far left ramp when lit after hitting center standup and completing standups/satellite requirements. During multiball, hit the right-center white standup to build jackpot values up to 100M. Jackpot values change during multiball, so timing is crucial.

**Goldeneye Wizard Mode:** Five-ball multiball after completing all 007 Encounters, Q's Pen, and spelling both 007 and GOLDENEYE. Scores 3M per shot and 20M per ramp.

## Key Tips
- **Play Satellite all day** - it's the highest-scoring feature in the game
- Tank Multiball can be aborted by pulling gun trigger to go for Satellite instead
- Hit all green guard standups to build satellite value before starting multiball
- GOLDENEYE hurry-ups start at 50M and count down 1M per second to 20M
- Completing all three GOLDENEYE hurry-ups raises the pop-up ramp for satellite access
- **Q's Pen mode is extremely difficult** - build multiple "eject or die" ball saves from video mode before attempting
- Video mode: shoot left side gun targets, pull trigger to shoot villains for ball saves
- Both multiballs may be available on ball 3 if you haven't played either yet
- Left ramp → Satellite combo doubles the combo value
- Avoid dangerous shots to Q's Pen unless you have multiple ball saves ready
