## About the Table
Gorgar is a 1979 Williams solid-state machine famous for being the first talking pinball game. This early electronic table features drop targets, a unique snake pit saucer, spinner, and classic layout with simple but effective scoring mechanics that reward both target shooting and lane management.

## Skill Shots
There is no traditional plunger skill shot on Gorgar. Instead, focus on controlled entry into play by plunging to trap up and assess the playfield state before making your first strategic shot.

## Early Game Strategy
Start each ball by assessing the snake pit value and bonus level. If bonus is below 10,000, work on advancing it through the A, C, D, E rollovers (1 advance each) or the B rollover (3 advances) to light the spinner at 10,000 bonus. If you're skilled at shatzing (alley passing), use the inlanes immediately for safe 3,000-point scoring and bonus advancement. Otherwise, begin working the GOR drop targets to advance snake pit value while staying away from dangerous shots.

## Mid Game Strategy
The primary mid-game strategy depends on your skill level:

**Advanced Strategy (Shatzing):** Master the alley pass technique to repeatedly hit the inlanes for 3,000 points each while advancing bonus and completing D and E rollovers. This spots 1-2-3-4 numbers, and completing 1-2-3-4 lights the snake pit for 50,000 points. Skilled players can build bonus to maximum (29,000) and light the snake pit multiple times through pure shatzing.

**Standard Strategy:** Focus on completing GOR drop targets to advance snake pit value, then collect the pit when lit for good value. Complete GAR targets to light pop bumpers (worth 1,000 per hit when lit vs 100 when unlit). Use the spinner window when bonus reaches 10,000-20,000 for additional scoring.

## Late Game Strategy
Prioritize collecting any lit snake pit value, especially if 20,000 or higher. Complete ABC rollovers for bonus multiplier (max 3x). With maximum bonus of 29,000 x 3x = 87,000, this becomes a significant end-of-ball score. If skilled at shatzing, continue working inlanes safely. Otherwise, play conservatively and avoid risky shots that could end your ball prematurely.

## Multiballs
Gorgar does not feature multiball play. Focus on single-ball strategy optimization.

## Key Tips
- **Snake pit resets:** If snake pit is lit for 20,000 or higher, it resets to lower value when collected
- **1-2-3-4 instant max:** Completing 1-2-3-4 in any order immediately lights snake pit for 50,000
- **Spinner window:** Spinner is only lit between 10,000-20,000 bonus - use this scoring opportunity
- **GAR target value:** When all 3 pop bumpers are lit (2-3 GAR completions), each GAR drop target scores 5,000 points
- **Shatzing technique:** On machines with inlane rubbers, alley passing is extremely safe and profitable
- **Bonus advancement:** B rollover gives 3 bonus advances vs 1 for other rollovers
- **Pop bumper lighting:** Each GAR completion lights one additional pop bumper for 1,000 per hit
- **Extra ball:** Complete GOR-GAR targets three times total to light extra ball
