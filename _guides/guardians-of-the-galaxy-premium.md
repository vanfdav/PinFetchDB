## About the Table
Guardians of the Galaxy Premium is a 2017 Stern pinball machine featuring an interactive Groot toy with motorized jaw, an Orb shot that triggers chaotic multiball, and a scoring system heavily focused on multiplier stacking. The game rewards strategic mode stacking during multiballs and proper use of the Hadron Enforcer system.

## Skill Shots
**Plunge into the flashing top lane for a basic Skill Shot.** The machine auto-plunges at a predetermined strength, so you cannot control plunge power. Focus on lighting the correct lane through nudging if needed. The skill shot provides a modest point award and contributes to overall lane completion bonuses.

## Early Game Strategy
**Start with Quill's Quest if unfamiliar with the machine** - select it before plunging ball one to get an immediate 2-ball multiball for safe exploration and Hadron collection. Otherwise, **prioritize completing white standups (5 Hadron Enforcer targets) which awards 3 Hadrons** - these are crucial for spotting shots later via the action button. **Collect Hadrons early and often** as they become essential for completing difficult shots during modes and multiballs.

## Mid Game Strategy
**Always bring a mode into your multiballs** by shooting the right scoop before starting multiball. **The key scoring strategy is multiplier stacking**: place shot multipliers on Groot (2X), start Groot multiball and lock 2 balls for 3X (now 6X), then start Rocket's 2X scoring (making Groot 12X). **Complete modes during multiballs when possible** - particularly good for switch-hit modes like Antiquities Level 2 or Yaka Arrow. **Use the smart action button to complete missing shots** in modes using your stored Hadrons.

## Late Game Strategy
**Focus on completing level 2 modes, especially Quill** - Level 2 Quill is a hurry-up worth 10% of your current score, making it extremely valuable late in the game. **Stack multipliers aggressively** as the scoring becomes exponential. **Complete 3 modes for first Extra Ball, 5 modes for second Extra Ball**. Avoid risky shots unless multipliers are in play - the base scoring without multipliers is relatively low.

## Multiballs
**Groot Multiball**: Lock 3 balls in Groot's mouth. During multiball, **put balls back in Groot for 2X then 3X playfield multipliers**. After first Groot MB, it takes 2 shots to open Groot and 4 locks for the next one. Use this primarily for **setting up shot multipliers and collecting Hadron Enforcers**.

**Orb Multiball**: Lock 3 balls in the Orb. **During multiball, shoot the Orb repeatedly for big points**. The chaotic nature makes ball control difficult but scoring potential is high.

**Cherry Bomb Multiball**: **Lit after completing 4 modes fully**. This is a wizard-level multiball with significant scoring opportunities.

**Multiball Stacking**: Both Groot and Orb Multiballs count as "modes" toward Extra Ball qualification and Cherry Bomb qualification.

## Key Tips
- **Complete 5 white Hadron Enforcer standups for 3 Hadrons** - always prioritize this
- **Use action button strategically** to spot difficult shots during modes and multiballs
- **Multiplier stacking is everything** - base scoring is low, multiplied scoring is massive
- **Play Escape Kyln during multiball** - requires 7 consecutive shots, much easier with multiple balls
- **Avoid starting Quill's Quest except as safety measure** - better to save for late-game scoring
- **The backhand to scoop technique**: slow ball on raised right flipper can be backhanded to scoop via quick double-tap
- **Save Xandar wizard** requires completing all modes 100% plus super jackpots in both multiballs
- **Right scoop starts modes** - always hit this before starting multiballs for mode stacking
- **Groot MB becomes setup multiball** after first completion - use for positioning multipliers
