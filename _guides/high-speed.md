## About the Table
High Speed (Williams, 1986) is a groundbreaking Steve Richie design featuring a cop chase theme. This classic game introduced the first pinball jackpot and established the modern ramp-centric playfield design. Key features include a main ramp, three flippers, multiball with jackpot, spinning red beacon, and the iconic "He got away!" callout.

## Skill Shots
The best skill shot is plunging up the ramp at game start. Before plunging, bump the plunger and wait for the tachometer lights to settle, then plunge with full power up the ramp. The ramp will spot a target in the traffic light sequence, giving you a head start on lighting multiball. This is much safer and more valuable than attempting to hit specific targets with the plunge.

## Early Game Strategy
Start each ball by shooting the ramp repeatedly to build bonus multiplier (2X to 5X) and advance toward multiball qualification. The ramp also spots standups safely without risk. Focus on completing the green, yellow, then red traffic light sequence by shooting the ramp and eject hole rather than the dangerous standup targets directly. Light the kickback early by shooting lit 1-6 targets or using alley passes to hit the lit inlanes.

## Mid Game Strategy
Once multiball is qualified (red light completed), shoot the ramp to lock the first ball. **Crucially, plunge the second ball at full speed up the ramp** - failing to do this results in only 2-ball multiball instead of 3-ball. During 3-ball multiball, immediately shoot the ramp for the jackpot (starts at 250K, increases to maximum 2M). After collecting the jackpot, focus on safe spinner shots and freeway collection. The spinners are worth significant points when lit (1K per spin after collecting two freeway values).

## Late Game Strategy
Maximize bonus collection by shooting the ramp 5 times on a single ball to achieve maximum bonus and hold bonus. Focus on freeway shots to light spinners and inlanes for safer scoring. If multiball is available, prioritize that over risky standup shots. Use post-transfer techniques to keep control and avoid dangerous areas of the playfield.

## Multiballs
**Getaway Multiball**: Complete green-yellow-red traffic lights, then shoot ramp to lock first ball. Plunge second ball at full speed up ramp for 3-ball multiball (2-ball if you miss). During multiball, shoot ramp immediately for jackpot. After jackpot, focus on spinners and freeway shots for safe scoring. The jackpot starts at 250K and can increase to 2M maximum.

## Key Tips
- **Always plunge the second multiball ball at full speed** - missing gives only 2-ball multiball
- **Ramp and eject hole both spot standups** - much safer than shooting targets directly
- **5 ramp shots on one ball gives maximum bonus and hold bonus**
- **Two lit freeway shots light all spinners for 1K per spin** - excellent safe scoring
- **Re-light kickback via lit 1-6 targets or alley-passing lit inlanes**
- **After jackpot in multiball, spinners and freeways are zero-risk points**
- **Avoid shooting standup targets directly** - use ramp and eject hole instead
- **Game uses automatic replay percentaging** - extra ball/replay scores adjust to player skill
