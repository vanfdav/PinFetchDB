## About the Table
John Wick is a 2024 Stern Pinball machine themed around the legendary assassin franchise. Designed by Elliot Eismin with dynamic AI-controlled enemy lights, the table features multiple multiballs, job modes, adversary battles, and unique mechanical features including a weapons crate and car target. The Premium model includes additional multiball modes and enhanced artwork.

## Skill Shots
Two skill shots are available: the **Baba Yaga skill shot** at the crate and the **Red Circle skill shot** at the back entrance. For tournament play, focus on the **Baba Yaga skill shot** - soft plunge avoiding both rollovers to hit just one crate switch for 1.5 million points and immediate job progression. Avoid soft plunges that go past the second rollover and roll back into the crate, as these provide minimal scoring and waste ball save time without advancing job progress.

## Early Game Strategy
Start each ball by attempting the Baba Yaga skill shot for 1.5M points. Immediately focus on **crate progression** to start your first job - hit the crate target repeatedly to advance through the weapon sequence. The crate bank shot into the right scoop is particularly effective and can register two crate hits with a single shot. Use ski jump passes to return the ball to the left flipper and repeat until job start is qualified. Begin with jobs that offer assassination goals for maximum scoring potential.

## Mid Game Strategy
Prioritize **job completion** for substantial scoring. During jobs, target lit shots with enemies in front of them for shot multipliers. Make five different shots (Oath Marker, Dance Club, Car, Continental, and Crate) to light the Administration scoop for an **Escape Jackpot worth approximately 20% of points scored during the battle**. The **right orbit** is exceptionally fast and safe for building towards multiball qualification. Spell WINSTON at the captive ball to light locks for Deconsecrated Multiball at the center ramp.

## Late Game Strategy
Focus on **car multiball stacking** for maximum scoring. Build the car jackpot value through repeated car shots before starting multiball. During Car Chase multiballs, jackpots start at the current car jackpot value and can be multiplied up to 4X. Car Super Jackpots are always 5X the base car jackpot value. Use the Administration scoop strategically during adversary battles - the escape jackpot becomes more valuable as you score more points during the battle.

## Multiballs
- **Car Chase Multiballs**: Left orbit, then smash car multiple times, then left orbit again. Choose **Helipad Showdown** (far left) or **Bagarre** (far right) for best scoring. Car jackpots multiply up to 4X, super jackpots are 5X base value.
- **Deconsecrated Multiball**: Spell WINSTON at captive ball, then lock balls at center ramp. More controlled multiball with consistent scoring opportunities.
- **Excommunicado Multiball**: Advanced multiball mode with enhanced scoring multipliers.

## Key Tips
- **Soft plunge strategy**: Try soft-plunging balls from trough before auto-launch to serve directly to right flipper via crate
- **Oath Marker targets**: Light outlane ball saves (flashing = active, solid = consumed). Fight adversaries to reset both outlanes
- **Job time extensions**: Job shots give 5 seconds extra time. Complete crate sequence again during each job for ~30 second extension
- **Car multiball selection**: When lit, shoot car to cycle between different multiball types - choose Helipad or Bagarre
- **Right orbit**: Fastest orbit shot on any modern Stern - use for safe, repeatable scoring
- **Enemy multipliers**: Always prioritize shots with enemies in front for multiplied values
- **Crate double-hits**: Bank shots off crate into right scoop count as two crate hits
