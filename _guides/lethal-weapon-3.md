## About the Table
Lethal Weapon 3 is a 1992 Data East pinball machine based on the action movie starring Mel Gibson and Danny Glover. The table features movie audio clips, a gun handle launcher, multiple ramps, drop targets, and a strong emphasis on the left "freeway" ramp for scoring progression. Known for its relatively simple ruleset but deep scoring potential through ramp combinations and multiball stacking.

## Skill Shots
Pull the gun trigger when the moving target is lit in the crosshairs on the dot matrix display. The skill shot awards points and advances the freeway ramp counter. This is a fairly forgiving skill shot with good reward - focus on timing rather than plunge strength. Missing the skill shot isn't catastrophic, but landing it consistently gives you a scoring advantage throughout the game.

## Early Game Strategy
Start every ball by shooting the left freeway ramp repeatedly. The ramp awards increasing millions (3M at 3 shots, 7M at 7 shots, continuing at 14, 21, 31, 41, etc.). This is the safest and most repeatable scoring method on the table. Avoid the drop targets early unless multiball is already one bank away from being lit. Focus on building your freeway ramp count as this underlies most major scoring opportunities later in the ball.

## Mid Game Strategy
Continue hammering the left ramp for escalating millions - this is your bread and butter scoring. The ramp becomes worth 10 million per shot during Looping mode (timed mode triggered by ramp shots). Watch for the replay light - when you earn a replay, immediately shoot the ramp for 25 million points. Work on completing Lethal Weapon 1-2-3 shots in any order to start modes worth 5/10/15/20/25 million each. The LW3 saucer (right of right orbit) gives random awards including Super Leo, which can award 10-30 million for Bank Heist.

## Late Game Strategy
If multiball is lit or one drop target bank away, go for it immediately - the quadruple jackpot is the game's highest single scoring opportunity. Otherwise, continue ramp shooting for consistent scoring. During bonus countdown, trap up and play safe unless you're behind and need to take risks. The game's generous ramp scoring means conservative play often wins tournaments.

## Multiballs
**Lotsaballs Multiball**: Light by completing either drop target bank (center playfield or lower right). Start at the VUK hole (upper center). During multiball, ramp is lit for 10M jackpot (increases by 5M per collect). Get double jackpot by shooting ramp twice quickly, or shoot lit saucer then ramp within 20 seconds. For quadruple jackpot, shoot lit saucer then make two quick ramp shots. Ball 3 always has multiball ready at the LW2 shot. Focus on jackpot progression rather than keeping all balls alive.

## Key Tips
- **After earning a replay, shoot the ramp immediately for 25 million points**
- **The left freeway ramp is your primary scoring method - shoot it relentlessly**
- **During video mode, shoot when the cow appears on screen for maximum points**
- **In mode completion videos, pull the trigger when character eyes flash for extra million**
- **Spinners fill the Uzi clip - empty via gun trigger for awards up to 6M**
- **Super Leo awards from LW3 saucer can be worth 10-30 million for Bank Heist**
- **During Crazy Riggs mode, hit anything for 250K per switch (especially spinners)**
- **Never consciously try to light multiball early - focus on ramp scoring instead**
- **Keep balls in pop bumpers during multiball for 100K-1M per hit during Super modes**
