## About the Table
South Park is a 1999 Sega pinball machine based on the animated TV series, notable for being Sega's final pinball game before the division was sold to form Stern Pinball. The game features character-based modes, multiball via the toilet mechanism, and distinctive crude humor with optional family-friendly language settings.

## Skill Shots
The skill shot targets rotating characters including Starvin Marvin, Officer Barbrady, Mayor McDaniels, Cartman's mother, and Stan's grandfather. **A successful skill shot is worth 5 million points**. Use a soft plunge for better control - you can still switch characters after plunging the ball to allow more reaction time for targeting the lit character.

## Early Game Strategy
Focus on lighting and starting character modes immediately. Each character mode completion lights their shot for additional jackpots during multiball, so prioritize at least killing Kenny before starting multiball as this is typically the easiest mode to complete. The character shots are: Cartman (scoop), Kenny (center target), Kyle (right ramp), Stan (left orbit), and Chef (lane behind lowered door).

## Mid Game Strategy
Character modes are the primary scoring engine. Kyle's mode scores **5M, 10M, 15M, then 20M** for four right ramp shots in 20 seconds. Chef's mode requires hitting all 5 character shots once each in 25 seconds, scoring **5M, 6M, 7M, 8M, and 10M** respectively. Kenny modes involve hitting his pop-up target for substantial points. Stack character mode completions before starting multiball - **each completed character lights their shot for double jackpot bonuses during multiball**.

## Late Game Strategy
If you've completed multiple characters, prioritize multiball for maximum scoring potential. The toilet multiball offers the highest single-ball scoring opportunity. With character completions, jackpots become worth significantly more. Avoid risky shots late in the ball unless you're behind - the bumper area can be dangerous for right outlane drains.

## Multiballs
**Toilet Multiball**: Light by hitting targets in front of the toilet, then start at the right ramp. The objective is to shoot the toilet 6 times initially to open it for a super jackpot. **Any completed character modes award double jackpot bonuses during multiball**, making character completion before multiball start crucial for maximum scoring.

## Key Tips
- **Soft plunge for skill shot control** - you can change characters after plunging
- **Complete at least Kenny before multiball** for guaranteed double jackpots
- **Character completions are worth 2x jackpots in multiball** - prioritize multiple character modes
- **Right ramp (Kyle) and left orbit are the safest repeatable million-point shots**
- **Toilet targets light multiball** - focus on these when character modes aren't lit
- **Bumpers can be profitable but risky** - they can kick balls to right outlane
- **Chef mode has tight 25-second timer** but offers escalating point values
- **Starting all characters lights mini wizard mode**, completing all lights 2x wizard mode
