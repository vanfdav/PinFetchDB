## About the Table
Taxi is a 1988 Williams solid state machine themed around driving a taxi through city streets. The game features a unique two-ramp layout with passengers to pick up, a two-ball multiball, and the iconic "airport million" scoring opportunity. Known for its straightforward rules but deep strategic scoring potential.

## Skill Shots
The skill shot involves plunging the ball into the right ramp funnel where it whirlpools around, advancing the award with each revolution. Awards progress from 1K to 5K, 10K, 25K, 50K, 75K, and 100K. The **25K + Pickup Passenger is the optimal skill shot** - it provides solid points plus picks up a passenger (usually Pinbot), advancing you toward jackpot. The skill shot multiplier increases with each ball (25K becomes 50K on ball 2, 75K on ball 3), making later balls more valuable. Plunge with medium strength to get multiple spins without going too fast.

## Early Game Strategy
Start each ball by attempting the 25K + Pickup Passenger skill shot. After the skill shot, immediately focus on **locking a ball in the right saucer** to enable multiball. The right saucer always locks the ball when hit. Avoid risky shots early - the key is getting to multiball quickly and safely. Pick up passengers when convenient but don't take dangerous shots for them initially.

## Mid Game Strategy
The core strategy revolves around **staying in multiball as much as possible**. Once you have a ball locked, shoot the left ramp to release it and start multiball. During multiball, all scores are doubled and you can collect jackpots. The jackpot shot requires hitting the Gorbie gate then the Santa saucer (or vice versa - both directions work). After multiball ends, immediately pick up another passenger to relight the right saucer for another lock. Build up the Airport value by shooting the left ramp when lit (via right inlane) - this progresses toward the valuable Airport Million.

## Late Game Strategy
Focus on collecting the **Airport Million** when lit by completing CAB three times on a single ball and lighting the extra ball at the DRAC shot. The left ramp scores the current airport value when lit. Complete CAB sequences to advance the end-of-ball bonus multiplier (2x, 3x, 4x, 5x). Subsequent CAB completions beyond 5x score 50K each. In multiball, attempt the dangerous but rewarding 250K crossover bonus by shooting both ramps simultaneously.

## Multiballs
Taxi has one two-ball multiball. Lock a ball in the right saucer, then shoot the left ramp to release and start multiball. During multiball, all scoring is doubled. The primary strategy is to **collect jackpots by hitting Gorbie gate followed by Santa saucer** (or reverse order). Keep one ball cradled for control while shooting jackpots with the other. The rare crossover bonus (250K) is achieved by shooting both ramps simultaneously during multiball - cradle one ball on each flipper and shoot both ramps at once.

## Key Tips
- **Always be in multiball** - this is the fundamental Taxi strategy
- The 25K + Pickup skill shot is the best risk/reward ratio
- Right saucer always locks a ball when hit
- Jackpot can be collected in either direction: Gorbie→Santa or Santa→Gorbie
- After multiball ends, pick up a passenger to relight the lock
- Skill shot values multiply by ball number (100K skill shot = 300K on ball 3)
- Light CAB three times on one ball to enable extra ball at DRAC shot
- Crossover bonus (250K) requires precise timing but huge reward
- Airport million is the ultimate scoring goal - build airport value via left ramp
- Carry Passenger target lights after completing Marilyn or Pinbot targets
- Be warned in multiplayer - other players can steal your locked ball
