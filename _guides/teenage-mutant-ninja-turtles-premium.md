## About the Table
Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles Premium is a 2020 Stern pinball machine themed around the classic 1987 animated series. The Premium version features an interactive Turtle Van for multiball locks and the Glider diverter assembly above the ramps for ball control. The machine focuses on episode-based modes, multiple multiball types, and scoring multipliers that can dramatically increase point values throughout play.

## Skill Shots
The skill shot changes based on your selected turtle character. Hit the corresponding turtle target for your character to light the Weapon Hurry-Up feature, which allows you to level up your turtle. Donatello players get an additional benefit - the skill shot also provides a swing at relighting Turtle Power multiball on ball launch. The skill shot is relatively safe and provides meaningful progression, making it worth attempting consistently.

## Early Game Strategy
Choose Donatello at game start - this immediately lights Turtle Power multiball at the right ramp with just one shot instead of four. Focus on hitting your turtle skill shot on plunge, then immediately go for the right ramp to start Turtle Power multiball. During multiball, prioritize hitting the four turtle shots plus the right ramp again to get add-a-ball. Complete LAIR at the left standup targets early to light Training modes and increase all scoring by 10% (maxing at 30% after three completions). Begin working toward Pizza multiball by shooting the van to lock balls and toppings.

## Mid Game Strategy
The core strategy revolves around stacking scoring opportunities. Get 2X playfield multiplier running as soon as possible through Training completion, then focus on Ninja Pizza multiball. Lock octopus/squid as a pizza topping whenever possible - this transforms the multiball into a spinner-focused scoring frenzy. During Ninja Pizza multiball, shoot blue shots to light Mondo Jackpot at the loop, then collect Mondo to light Grande Jackpot at the Lair (Splinter shot). Complete episodes strategically - focus on easier ones early, saving harder episodes for when scoring multipliers are active.

## Late Game Strategy
After completing four episodes, Team-Up multiball becomes available at the right ramp and provides significant scoring with completed episode perks. If approaching eight completed episodes, Final Battle wizard mode awards massive points based on accumulated episode values (taking 2X scoring into this mode is extremely lucrative). Conservative trap-up play becomes more important as ball times increase. Focus on controlled shots to high-value targets rather than risky combo attempts.

## Multiballs
**Turtle Power Multiball**: Start at right ramp (1 shot for Donatello, 4 for others). Hit four turtle shots plus right ramp for add-a-ball. Provides solid base scoring but not the highest point potential.

**Ninja Pizza Multiball**: Lock three balls at the van, locking in five toppings total (2+2+1). Octopus topping is crucial - makes spinner shots extremely valuable. Blue shots light Mondo Jackpot at loop, then hit loop to light Grande Jackpot at Splinter shot.

**Team-Up Multiball**: Available after four completed episodes. Each completed episode adds perks to the multiball, making episode completion order important for maximizing this mode's potential.

## Key Tips
- Choose Donatello for instant multiball access and easier skill shot benefits
- Octopus pizza topping transforms Ninja Pizza multiball into highest-scoring opportunity
- Complete Training modes early for 10-30% scoring increases on everything
- The right ramp is safe and repeatable - use for Turtle Power multiball and later Team-Up
- Backhand shots are viable to both right ramp and center shots with practice
- Take 2X playfield multiplier into Final Battle wizard mode for maximum points
- Re-light multiballs by playing through four episodes each time
- Target time settings can extend first ball to three minutes - useful for learning
- Avoid the dangerous shots under the flippers unless trapping perfectly to control timing
