## About the Table
Venom is a 2023 Stern pinball machine themed around Marvel's symbiote anti-hero. Designed by Brian Eddy with code by Dwight Sullivan, the game features host selection that fundamentally alters gameplay, vertical symbiote containment vessels for ball locks, and mechanically changing pathways. The Premium model includes additional features like Million Plus pops and enhanced multiball modes.

## Skill Shots
Eddie Brock provides the best skill shot for tournament play. Set the plunge strength to hit "left of scoop" and make the combo shot for immediate progress toward mini-modes. This skill shot both completes a mini-mode and sets up early scoring opportunities. The standard "plunge into flashing lane" skill shot is available across all hosts but only awards 2 bonus multipliers, making it less valuable than character-specific skill shots.

## Early Game Strategy
Always select Eddie Brock as your host - he provides the most tournament-friendly scoring opportunities and two free lock shots. Focus on the flashing yellow, blue, and white shots to complete mini-modes quickly. Hit the skill shot combo, then work on building your mini-mode ladder. Avoid risky shots early and establish a foundation by completing at least 3-4 mini-modes before attempting multiball.

## Mid Game Strategy
The core tournament strategy revolves around multiball stacking. Lock 3 balls for Mayhem Multiball, preferably using Eddie's free lock benefits. During Mayhem MB, hit the Carnage target repeatedly until the Police Station (center ramp) lights red, then shoot it to stack Carnage Multiball. This combination should easily net 100-110 million points. During stacked multiballs, hold the action button to activate Spider-Sense and hit random flashing shots to light super jackpots. Focus on consecutive jackpot shots to build multipliers (2x, 3x, 4x progression).

## Late Game Strategy
After successful multiball stacking, consider switching to Gwenom for enhanced Infected scoring and doppelganger hurry-ups if you've filled Eddie's mini-mode ladder. Work toward starting Toxin Team-Up wizard mode if time permits. Focus on safe shots to preserve your ball - the Alchemax ramp and Daily Bugle can both be backhanded for safer approaches. Avoid the Life Foundation building area if the ball tends to get stuck during ball search.

## Multiballs
**Mayhem Multiball**: Primary scoring mode. Start after completing enough mini-modes. Stack with Carnage for maximum points.
**Carnage Multiball**: Stack with Mayhem by hitting Carnage target during Mayhem MB, then shooting red-lit center ramp. Adds significant scoring to base multiball.
**Quick Doppelganger Multiball** (Premium/LE): Available through specific host abilities. Use Hulk for enhanced scoring - when Quick Jackpot phase ends, use action button for Hulk Smash (7x JP + light Super JP).

## Key Tips
- Choose Eddie Brock for tournament play due to free lock benefits and scoring consistency
- Lock 3 balls and stack Mayhem + Carnage multiballs for 100M+ scoring opportunities
- Start Mayhem before Carnage for optimal stacking (per PinTips community advice)
- Cross your arms when flippers are reversed during Grendel battles (left hand on right flipper, right hand on left flipper)
- Use Spider-Sense (hold action button) during multiballs to light super jackpots at random flashing shots
- Both Alchemax ramp and Daily Bugle can be backhanded for safety
- Wait for at least 4 balls before starting multiball if possible for maximum multiplier potential
- Avoid the Life Foundation building if ball search issues are present - be ready to nudge
- Million Plus pops (Premium feature) should be prepped to 1-3 hits away before starting multiball modes
