# Cactus Canyon — Tournament Strategy Guide

## About the Table
Cactus Canyon is a 1998 Bally pinball machine with a Wild West theme featuring sheriffs, outlaws, and multiballs. The game centers around building toward the High Noon wizard mode while managing multiple scoring opportunities through Quick Draws, multiballs, and bonus multipliers. The table is known for its deep ruleset and potential for massive bonus scores.

## Skill Shots
The skill shot awards cycle through various valuable options including Lock, Quick Draw, 1M points, and Bonus X+3. Pay attention to the currently selected award before plunging - a soft plunge allows you to collect the displayed award without hitting the skill shot switch. The most valuable awards are Lock (saves time building toward multiball), Quick Draw (starts immediate scoring mode), and Bonus X+3 (crucial for end-of-ball scoring). Time the plunge to land on high-value awards rather than settling for the default.

## Early Game Strategy
Start by establishing bonus multiplier progression through the upper left rollovers - shoot the left orbit which feeds these lanes for the first 3 shots. Focus on lighting and completing Quick Draws through the skill shot or side spot targets, as these build toward Showdown multiball and increase your rank (higher ranks mean higher Quick Draw values - Marshall starts at 2M vs Stranger at 500k). Begin building toward Gold Mine multiball by hitting the mine to light locks. Avoid risky shots like center ramps early unless specifically needed for mode progression.

## Mid Game Strategy
Prioritize Gold Mine multiball as your primary scoring engine. Lock 3 balls, then during multiball hit all 5 jackpot shots (3 ramps + 2 loops) worth 500k+ each. Completing all 5 shots relights them with a multiplier and adds 250k to the Motherlode value. Each set of 5 jackpots increases the Motherlode multiplier. Cash in the Motherlode at the mine for massive points (can reach infinite value with enough jackpot sets). Stack Showdown multiball by raising the 4th Quick Draw but not completing it, starting another multiball, then hitting the drop target to begin Showdown for unlimited add-a-balls (tournament ROM limits to 2).

## Late Game Strategy
Focus heavily on bonus multiplier during multiball play - shoot left orbit repeatedly during any multiball as the diverter stays closed, feeding the upper rollovers. Bonus can become enormous (24x max on tournament ROM). Complete Polly Peril for 5x bonus addition by hitting any ramp 3 times to start, then hitting left/right ramps 5 times at 500k each. Cash in large Motherlode values during Gold Mine multiball. Avoid unnecessary risks in the final moments - trap up and play safe shots to preserve bonus multiplier and ball time.

## Multiballs
**Gold Mine Multiball**: Hit mine to light lock, lock 3 balls. All major shots (3 ramps, 2 loops) lit for 500k+ jackpots. Completing all 5 relights them with multiplier and builds Motherlode. Cash Motherlode at mine during multiball only.

**Showdown Multiball**: Complete 4 Quick Draws to start. Begins as 2-ball, hit all 4 drop targets for 300k jackpots and add-a-ball (unlimited on original ROM, max 2 on tournament). Can be stacked with other multiballs.

**Stampede Multiball**: Hit all major shots 3 times each. All shots lit for 250k jackpots with one roving 500k shot. On tournament ROM, repeatedly hitting the roving shot without hitting others builds multiplier up to 6x (3M max).

**High Noon Wizard Mode**: Requires 10 combos, collected Motherlode, defeated 3 Bart Brothers, started Stampede MB, and started Showdown MB. 4-ball multiball with 30-second timer (+1 sec per bad guy killed, max 99). Score 1M per bad guy drop target hit.

## Key Tips
- **Left orbit is the safest shot** and feeds bonus multiplier rollovers when diverter is closed
- **During any multiball, focus on left orbit** for bonus X buildup since diverter stays closed
- **Watch for skill shot timing** - soft plunge can award valuable items without hitting the switch
- **In shootouts, watch all 4 bad guy targets** - the one that didn't flash is the one that pops up
- **Stack Showdown with other multiballs** by raising the 4th Quick Draw, starting another MB, then completing the drop
- **Motherlode can only be collected during Gold Mine multiball** - value resets to 250k after collection
- **Bonus multiplier is crucial** - can reach 24x on tournament ROM through upper rollovers and Polly Peril
- **Rank progression matters** - higher ranks mean higher Quick Draw starting values (Marshall 2M vs Stranger 500k)
- **Avoid center ramp** unless specifically needed - it's a dangerous shot with moderate rewards
- **Gunfights award 750k and increase rank** - ball stops in inlane, hit the protruding drop target
